Keynotes was established in 1969. By 1970, a scholarship fund was started with a donation in memory of Fay S. Olmsted. Since 1971, Keynotes Music Scholarship has awarded over $452,860.00 to 203 students!

About Us


We are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization located in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. Our mission is to offer music scholarships to full-time undergraduate and graduate music major students already enrolled in a music school or in the music department of a college or university.
