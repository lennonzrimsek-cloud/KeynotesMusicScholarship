About Us

We are a 501(c)3 non-profit organization located in Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. Our mission is to offer music scholarships to full-time undergraduate and graduate music major students already enrolled in a music school or in the music department of a college or university. The following members have been elected to our 2024-2026 Executive Board: Claudia Benack (Vice President) Margaret Zabo (Recording Secretary) Toni Kvederis (Corresponding Secretary) Lynda Hagan (Treasurer/Scholarship Treasurer)
