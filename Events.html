A Sampling of our Organization’s Many Activities

We have variety of activities throughout the year. Our year kicks off in May with the Scholarship Competition and Annual Dinner. In June, we meet for our Annual Business Meeting and Election. Our Annual Scholarship Fundraiser occurs in September. The final event for the year is the Fall Tea which highlights one of our scholarship winners.
